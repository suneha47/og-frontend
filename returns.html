Our Return Promise

We want you to love what you ordered. If there's an issue with your product, we're here to help. Please read our return policy carefully before initiating a return.

 We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for eligible products.

How to Return

1
Contact Us
WhatsApp or call us within 7 days of receiving your order
2
Share Details
Send your order ID, photos of the product, and reason for return
3
Approval
We'll review and confirm if your return is eligible within 24 hours
4
Refund / Exchange
Approved returns get a replacement or refund within 3–5 business days

Eligible for Return

 Returns are accepted for these reasons:

  • Product received is damaged or defective
  • Wrong item delivered (different from what was ordered)
  • Product is significantly different from the description
  • Product arrived with missing parts or accessories

Not Eligible for Return

 Returns are NOT accepted in these cases:

  • Return request made after 7 days of delivery
  • Product has been used, washed, or altered
  • Tags or original packaging have been removed
  • Damage caused by misuse, accidents, or improper care
  • Personal hygiene products (perfumes that have been opened and used)
  • Products purchased during final sale or clearance events

Refund Process

  • Approved refunds are processed within 3–5 business days
  • For COD orders — refund via UPI transfer to your provided UPI ID
  • For UPI / online payments — refund to the original payment method
  • Exchange for a different product of equal or greater value (difference to be paid)

Cancellation Policy

  • Orders can be cancelled before they are shipped — cancel from the "My Orders" section
  • Once shipped, orders cannot be cancelled (initiate a return after delivery instead)
  • Cancelled COD orders have no refund applicable
  • Cancelled prepaid orders will be refunded within 3–5 business days
